使用条款

订阅服务

您可以按月或年订阅该服务，并需要您按照订阅周期按月或年支付相应费用； 默认情况下您的订阅周期结束后系统会自动续费，您可以自行提前取消订阅。

取消订阅

如果您不再需要订阅服务，请最好在订阅到期前 24 小时进行取消操作； 您可以通过以下方法取消：1.打开“设置”-“Apple ID”-订阅-点击本软件，取消订阅即可； 2.打开 App-点击设置-点击订阅模块右上角设置按钮，取消订阅即可。

订阅费用

订阅费用可能会根据促销活动有所变化，也会因汇率或地区而不用，实际金额请以 App 展示的金额为准。

退款

订阅开始后无法进行退款，为避免损失，请务必在订阅到期前自行取消订阅。 在 App 设置页面的订阅模块下提供了退款操作，来对交易执行退款， 交易能否完成退款需要系统判定，为避免损失，请务必在订阅到期前自行取消订阅。

恢复购买

如果您已经成功订阅并支付了相关费用，但是订阅没有正常展示， 您可以在 APP 设置页面的订阅模块下点击“恢复购买”进行恢复。

订阅共享

当前订阅仅供个人使用，无法通过家庭组实现共享。

联系我们

如果您有任何疑问或建议，可以通过发送邮件到 minesaner@163.com； 感谢您的支持。

Terms of Use

You may subscribe to the Service on a monthly or yearly basis and will require you to pay a monthly or yearly fee for the subscription period; By default, your subscription period will automatically renew after the end of the subscription period, and you can cancel your subscription early.

Subscription cancellation

If you no longer need to subscribe to the service, it's best to cancel it 24 hours before it expires; You can cancel by the following methods: 1. Open "Settings" - "Apple ID" - Subscribe - tap this App and cancel the subscription; 2. Open this App - Tap Settings - Tap the Settings button in the upper right corner of the subscription section, and cancel the subscription.

Subscription fees

Subscription fees may vary based on promotions and may not be used due to exchange rates or regions, subject to the amount displayed in the app.

Refund

Refunds cannot be made after the subscription has started, so to avoid loss, be sure to cancel your subscription yourself before it expires. A refund action is provided under the subscription module on the App Settings page to perform a refund on the transaction. Whether the transaction can be completed and refunded needs to be determined by the system, in order to avoid losses, please be sure to cancel the subscription before the subscription expires.

Restore purchase

If you have successfully subscribed and paid for it, but the subscription does not show properly, You can restore it by clicking "Restore Purchase" button under the subscription module on the APP Settings page.

Subscription sharing

Current subscriptions are for personal use only and can't be shared through a homegroup.
